History

  • The New York Giants are a professional american football team established in 1925.
  • They play at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey. Their colors are dark blue, red and white.
  • The franchise has won 4 Super Bowl championships, with the most recent 2 being in 2007 and 2011, where both times they defeated the rival New England Patriots.
